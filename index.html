HOW IT WORKS

SYNW is a weekly film club where we get together and watch a film together in isolation. We try and find films we never knew existed or that we love enough to watch again. All you have to do is source a copy of the film, cue it up to play then, next Wednesday - 8:30 on the dot - pless PLAY.

There are Coming Distractions to watch before the film from 8:10pm and we'll be about in the chat from 8pm.

  1. Check the website, Instagram, Twitter or the WhatsApp group to see what the next film is
  2. Track a down a copy of the film. You can rent / sream from one of the Renting / Streaming Options
  3. Come Wednesday 8pm, get the website up, make popcorn, get comfy, chat on the group,
    watch the Coming Distractions etc
  4. At 8:20pm, cue up the film paused from the first frame and wait for the countdown
  5. Watch the countdown at the bottom of the website and, bang on zero, hit PLAY
