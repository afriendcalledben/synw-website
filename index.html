HOW IT WORKS

SYNW is a weekly film club where we get together and watch a film together in isolation. We try and find films we never knew existed or that we love enough to watch again. All you have to do is source a copy of the film, cue it up to play then, next Wednesday - 8:30 on the dot - pless PLAY.

There are Coming Distractions to watch before the film from 8:15pm and we'll be about in the chat from 8pm.

  1. Check the website, Twitter or group to see what the next film is.
  2. Track a down a copy of the film. Buy, rent or stream from one the options.
  3. Come Wednesday 8pm, get the website up, make popcorn, get comfy, chat on the group, watch the trailers etc
  4. At 8:20pm, cue up the film paused from the first frame and wait for the countdown
  5. Watch the countdown at the bottom of the website and hit PLAY
